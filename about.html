About Me

My name is Saira Arissya Nur binti Muhammad Adlizan. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Information Technology (Hons) in Computer System Security at Universiti Kuala Lumpur Malaysian Institute of Information Technology (UniKL MIIT).

My academic journey began in 2021, following my graduation from SMK Sri Hartamas with an SPM certification. Later that year, I enrolled in the Foundation in Information Technology programme at UniKL MIIT, which I completed over two years. This foundation strengthened my technical skills and led me to further pursue a degree in the same institution. I am now in the midst of completing my Bachelor of Computer Science (Hons.) in Cyber Security, a 3.5-year programme that has deepened both my technical and analytical capabilities.

Why Cybersecurity?

My interest in cybersecurity was sparked by a growing awareness of digital threats, particularly online scams and hacking. I found myself fascinated by how these attacks work, what makes them successful, and most importantly, how they can be prevented. This curiosity evolved into a purposeful academic path where I now study the technologies and strategies involved in protecting information systems.

Designing in the Digital Space

While my primary focus lies in cybersecurity, I also possess a strong appreciation for digital design. My interest in web design began during a course that introduced the fundamentals of front-end development. With a background in traditional art forms such as sketching and painting, I found digital design to be an extension of that creative expression, offering structure and interactivity to the visual process.

My Approach and Values

As a student and aspiring professional, I believe in continuous learning, adaptability, and precision. I approach every task with a sense of responsibility and a commitment to both clarity and quality. Whether solving a cybersecurity challenge or refining a user interface, I strive to balance technical accuracy with thoughtful execution.

Beyond the Classroom

Outside of my academic and technical interests, I enjoy reading and listening to music, activities that offer a peaceful escape from the often overwhelming pace of life. I also find clarity and reflection through walking, especially when I need to regain focus or unwind. These hobbies help me stay grounded and maintain balance as I navigate both academic demands and personal growth.

Looking Ahead

While I am still exploring specific career paths, I have developed a particular interest in cybersecurity forensics. The investigative nature of this field appeals to me deeply, aligning with my fascination for criminology and problem-solving. I look forward to contributing meaningfully in roles that blend technical expertise with critical analysis.
